Trigonometric Functions

f(x) = a(sin(b(x-h))) + k This is the general equation of a sin wave. A represents the vertical stretch of the sin wave. B represents the wavelength of the sin wave. H represents the horizontal shift of the sin wave. K represents the vertical shift of the sin wave. f(x) = a(cos(b(x-h))) + k This is the general equation of a cos wave. A represents the vertical stretch of the cos wave. B represents the wavelength of the cos wave. H represents the horizontal shift of the cos wave. K represents the vertical shift of the cos wave.
